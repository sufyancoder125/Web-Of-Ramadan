About Ramadan

What is Ramadan?

Ramadan is the ninth month of the Islamic calendar, observed by Muslims worldwide as a month of fasting, prayer, and reflection.

Fasting & Worship

Fasting from dawn to sunset is one of the Five Pillars of Islam. It helps in self-discipline, spiritual growth, and empathy for the less fortunate.
